# term
# about lipid
### fat
fat:
+ (n.) 脂肪
+ (adj.) 胖的

### obese
obese:
+ (adj.) 肥胖的 (更精確說是，BMI超過國際衛生組織的標準)
  
### obesity
obesity:
+ (n.) 肥胖 (`obese`的名詞形式)

### lipid
lipid:
+ (n.) 脂質

> [!TIP]
> 單字拆解
> 
> -lip- : fat (from Greek `lipos`, which means fat)

### lipogenic
lipogenic:
+ (adj.) 轉成脂肪的

> [!TIP]
> 單字拆解
> 
> -gen- : generate
> 
> -ic : a suffix for adjective

### pro-lipogenic
pro-lipogenic:
+ (adj.) 有利於轉成脂肪的

> [!TIP]
> 單字拆解
> 
> pro : advantage
>
> Think about the phrase `pros and cons` which means `advantage and disadvantages`.


### anti-lipogenic
anti-lipogenic:
+ (adj.) 不利於轉成脂肪的

> [!TIP]
> 單字拆解
> 
> anti- : against, back (here it means against)
